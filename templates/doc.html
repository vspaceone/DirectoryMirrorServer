Loading spaceapi

To load a space use 

        GET /spaces/:spacename:
The :spacename: variable is the urlencoded version of the name in the directory. E.g. %2Fdev%2Ftal.

Example: /spaces/%2Fdev%2Ftal
Load stats

Load 

        GET /stats/:spacename:.html
to show a stats site.

Example: stats/%2Fdev%2Ftal.html

Example: stats/%2Fdev%2Ftal.html
